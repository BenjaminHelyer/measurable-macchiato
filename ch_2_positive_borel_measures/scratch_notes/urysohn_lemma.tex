\section{Urysohn's Lemma}

\begin{theorem}[Uryson's Lemma]
Suppose $X$ is a locally compact Hausdorff space, $V$ is open in X, $K \subset V$, and $K$ is compact.
Then there exists an $f \in C_c(x)$, such that $K \preccurlyeq f \preccurlyeq V$.
\end{theorem}
